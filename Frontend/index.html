How to Play

Exploding Kittens is a strategic, fast-paced card game designed for 2-5 players. The goal of the game is simple: avoid drawing an Exploding Kitten card while using action cards to outsmart your opponents. Each player draws one card per turn, hoping to stay alive while gradually eliminating others from the game.

Players have a variety of cards at their disposal. For example, "Skip" lets you skip your turn, "Attack" forces the next player to take extra turns, and "See the Future" allows you to peek at upcoming cards. Using these cards wisely is key to survival, as they can prevent disaster or give you the upper hand against opponents.

Exploding Kittens adds humor and unpredictability to every round. If you draw an Exploding Kitten, you must immediately defuse it with a "Defuse" card or face elimination. This keeps tension high, as the deck gradually becomes more dangerous and strategic thinking becomes essential.

The last player left without exploding is declared the winner. While luck plays a role, careful planning, timing, and reading your opponents’ strategies are what separate the champions from the rest. Exploding Kittens combines chaos, strategy, and laughs in a way that makes every game exciting and unique.
